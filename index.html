Frequently Asked Questions

Is ConvertHub really free?

Yes! ConvertHub is 100% free with no hidden charges. There are no watermarks on your output files, and you don't need to create an account. All processing happens directly in your browser.

Are my files safe?

Absolutely. Your files never leave your device. All conversion and processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We don't upload or store any of your files on any server.

What file formats are supported?

ConvertHub supports JPG, PNG, WebP, BMP, GIF images, PDF documents, DOCX (Word) files, and PPTX (PowerPoint) files. You can convert between these formats, compress them, or edit images with our photo tools.

How do I compress a PDF to a specific size?

Open the PDF Compress tool, upload your PDF, and select your target file size (100KB, 200KB, 500KB, 1MB, or enter a custom size). ConvertHub will optimize the PDF to meet your target size while maintaining the best possible quality.

Can I create passport photos for any country?

Yes! Our Passport Photo Maker supports standard sizes for most countries including US (2×2 inches), UK/EU (35×45mm), India (2×2 inches), and more. You can also enter custom dimensions.

Is there a file size limit?

Since all processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's available memory. Most modern devices can handle files up to 100MB without issues. For very large files, we recommend processing them in batches.
